What is Round 1?

We are the official competitive video games club for James Madison University. We have weekly RanBats and tournaments every month. From Smash to Tekken 7 to Duck Game to Mario Cart, if you can compete against someone, we play it! From beginner to advanced, all levels of play are welcome in Round 1. Our goal is to keep the competitive video game scene bumping here in Harrisonburg. We love teaching new players! If you're even a bit curious just come by one of our meetings or join our Facebook group.
Weekly meetings are held Wednesday on campus in MU 404 from 7-10PM!

Where?

Madison Union 404
